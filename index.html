Did you know!

  • Chameleons are 4 inches long when they hatch and grow between 15 and 20 inches long nose to tail for adult male chameleons.
  • On average, a chameleon's tongue is roughly twice the length of its body. In humans, that would be a tongue about 10 to 12 feet long.
  • Chameleons frequently carry bacteria called Salmonella that can cause serious illness in people.
